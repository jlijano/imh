How to Request an Appointment

Call 320-03-15 to 19, use the contact form, or message the official Facebook page. Provide the concern type and preferred department.

Emergency Visit Instructions

For urgent or life-threatening concerns, call immediately or proceed to the emergency area. Do not wait for a website reply.

Admission Process

Bring a valid ID, physician referral or orders, prior results, medicines, PhilHealth/HMO documents, and emergency contact details.

Discharge Process

Coordinate with the attending physician, nursing station, billing, pharmacy, and records as instructed by hospital staff.
