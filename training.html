Training & Education

A teaching hospital shaped by service and clinical learning.

IMH's legacy includes nursing education, healthcare training, and residency programs serving the future of care in Western Visayas.

Clinical Training

Iloilo Mission Hospital supports clinical training for Medicine, Nursing, Medical Technology, Physical Therapy, Medical Social Work, Pharmacy, Nutrition and Dietetics, Chaplaincy, Business, and Information Technology.

Program details, affiliations, rotations, and requirements should be confirmed with official IMH training offices and partner institutions.
