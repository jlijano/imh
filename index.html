Private tertiary teaching hospital in Western Visayas

Caring for Iloilo Since 1901

Iloilo Mission Hospital is a trusted tertiary teaching hospital in Jaro, Iloilo City, serving patients and families through compassionate healthcare, holistic healing, and generations of clinical training.

Rooted in Christian service and strengthened by more than a century of care, IMH continues to serve the community with dedicated healthcare professionals, modern facilities, and a deep commitment to healing.

Heritage

A Legacy of Healing and Training

Iloilo Mission Hospital began in 1901 as a mission hospital dispensary and has grown into a major healthcare institution serving Iloilo and surrounding communities. Its story is closely tied to Christian service, nursing education, clinical training, and generations of patient care in Western Visayas.
