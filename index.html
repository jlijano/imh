Trusted care since 1901

A hospital built around service, learning, and community care.

Iloilo Mission Hospital is widely known as IMH and CPU-Iloilo Mission Hospital. Public sources and Central Philippine University history describe IMH as part of the early mission hospital work in Iloilo in 1901, with a continuing teaching hospital role.

For current clinical availability, visiting rules, training inquiries, insurance processing, or department schedules, please contact the hospital directly.
