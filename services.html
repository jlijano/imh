Services

Hospital services organized for easier patient routing.

Service availability, physician assignment, hours, and preparation requirements may change. Please contact IMH directly before visiting.

Medical

Adult medical care and specialty evaluation, including internal medicine, heart, lung, kidney, neurology, dermatology, psychiatry, rheumatology, immunology, and allergy-related care areas listed in current site materials.

Surgical

Surgical consultation and care pathways, including general surgery, laparoscopic surgery, ENT, ophthalmology, neurosurgery, cardiovascular and thoracic surgery, urology, orthopedics, and hand surgery.

Women and Children

Care areas for OB-Gynecology, Pediatrics, neonatal intensive care, and pediatric intermediate or intensive care. Please confirm schedules and requirements directly.

Diagnostics

Clinical pathology, laboratory medicine, blood bank, CT scan, radiology, ultrasound, endoscopy, bronchoscopy, stress test, echocardiography, electroencephalography, and colposcopy services may require physician requests or preparation steps.

Rehabilitation

Physical therapy and rehabilitation medicine support patients working toward recovery, mobility, and functional improvement. Ask the hospital about referrals and schedules.

Special Care

Pain management, palliative care clinic, hospice care, chaplaincy service, ambulance service, and dental service are listed care areas. Please contact IMH to confirm current availability and routing.

Support Services

Pharmacy, nutrition clinic, billing, records, admissions, and related support offices help patients complete the steps around care. Requirements should be confirmed with the appropriate office.

Need help choosing a department?

Call IMH or use the contact page for routing. Do not use the website for emergency triage or treatment advice.
