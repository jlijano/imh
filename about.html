About IMH

A heritage of care in Iloilo since 1901.

Iloilo Mission Hospital serves patients and healthcare learners from its home in Jaro, Iloilo City.

History and Identity

Central Philippine University history records that Baptists and Presbyterians partnered in operating Iloilo Mission Hospital in 1901. The hospital is also known publicly as IMH and CPU-Iloilo Mission Hospital.

IMH's story is closely connected with Christian service, nursing education, clinical training, and community healthcare in Western Visayas.
