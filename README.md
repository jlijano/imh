# Iloilo Mission Hospital Website

Static responsive website for Iloilo Mission Hospital, also known as IMH or CPU-Iloilo Mission Hospital.

## Pages

- Home
- About IMH
- Services
- Doctors & Departments
- Patient Guide
- Training & Education
- News & Updates
- Contact

## Editing Notes

This site is intentionally built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript so hospital staff or maintainers can edit content without a build step.

Admin-editable content areas include:

- Services and department listings in `services.html`
- Placeholder doctor directory cards in `doctors.html`
- News and announcement cards in `news.html`
- Contact details and form options in `contact.html`

Doctor names, clinic schedules, insurance/HMO details, PhilHealth guidance, visiting hours, official map embed code, and Facebook feed integration should be confirmed by authorized Iloilo Mission Hospital staff before publication.

## Compliance

The site includes a general health information disclaimer and avoids diagnosis or treatment advice. Emergency information is kept visible across pages.
